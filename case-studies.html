Case Studies

As a brand strategist, Patron Sainte helps brands understand what is at their core. Often these are young companies with an audience who have identified a need to refine their voice and visuals. Projects begin with listening, identifying audience & archetypes, and writing a brief. Depending on the brand's needs, the projects may include logo and identity design, web, video, or some combination of those.
