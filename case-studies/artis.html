Artís Case Study

Artís is a coffee roastery and cafe in San Francisco that roasts in small batches to sustain peak freshness. They came to our team for web and video help. We delved into the brand, considered their core values, rally cry, customer needs, and digital experience.

01 Brand

The target demographics for Artís are Persons of Particulars: the Gourmet, the Cultural Explorer, and the Connesiuer/Craft Enthusiast.

The archetypes that this brand embodies are the Explorer, Creator, and Storyteller. Artís is about freshness and flavor. At it's core, this brand is about seizing the moment, since coffee peaks in flavor 24-48 hours after it is roasted.

Photo by Jon Kline

02 Web and Copywriting

The typical customer for Artís is interested in what they are purchasing and especially in what they are consuming. They enjoy learning, so we gave them not only information about the cafe and how to roast, but also information about the history of coffee.

The voice for Artís is casual and educated. It is important to balance the information presented with a reminder of how it's relevant to your cup. So for example, in the How to Brew sections, we explain how to make coffee using a Chemex, but also why you might want to (for the curious, it yields a cup with a light body and clean taste).

03 Flavor Profile

Coffee is so commonplace that it might seem odd to think about it as having similar complexities as wine. But like wine, the growing regions and processing methods affect the flavor.

We included a flavor profile quiz to help non-coneisseurs talk about the flavors they enjoyed in coffee and those they didn't. The quiz yields results about the region and the roast that you might like. It was named by SF Weekly as one of the best they've seen.

05 Video

Our team conceptualized the hero video around the rally cry "Seize the Moment." We chose another ephemeral occurence - the feeling you get when you might be falling in love and have to make a play for it. We collaborated on a script and storyboards. I created a moodboard. During production, I served as art department, sourcing props, dressing sets, and managing wardrobe.
