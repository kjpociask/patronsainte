Milwaukee Ballet Case Study

The Milwaukee Ballet approached our team looking for help in reaching a younger audience and spreading the word about the caliber of their truly world-class talent. We produced a series of videos for the Milwaukee Ballet focusing on the lives and history of the leading artists of the Milwaukee Ballet.

01 Brand Strategy

The ballet has a number of partner creators producing individual pieces. These separate marketing efforts result in the need for unification. 'Move your soul' was at the core of our production and we hope it will be used by others.

02 Production

To prep for our meeting the dancers, we reviewed their bios and created a list of questions that would tease emotion and individuality out. On set, I did set decoration and made tweaks to lighting. For a few of the dancers, I did the interviewing.

03 Post Production

During post production, I directed the edit and motion graphics. I worked with a composer to achieve a musical score that mached the personality of the dancers. I also named the videos.

04 Promotion

The plan for promotion was carried out by the Ballet on Facebook and Twitter. Their efforts ran in conjunction with the ticket sales promotion for thier season finale. The dancers all played prominent roles, so it gave audiences a chance to see them as people as well as dancers, outside of their characters. Audiences enjoyed the videos, putting in requests for more featuring other dancers.
