Coast Polish Co. Case Study


Coast Polish Co. is a boutique custom blended nail polish line for young women that maintain a professional appearance and waint to show their fun side. Coast Polish Co. founder Chelsey Orlikowski was seeking a new look with a more robust brand style guide and design support for the future.

01 Naming

The original name of the company "CJ Laquer" was severe sounding. The hard consonance and the choice of the word 'laquer' evoked furniture-making. After discovering that the brand personality was fun and luxe, we decided to rename.

To get started brainstorming, we talked about favorite vacations, since this brand is about punctuating work with fun. Coast, a word that kept coming up in answers, evoked relaxation and beauty. When paired with 'Polish Co.' it became stately.

02 A New Logo

An inspirational brand for Coast Polish Co. is Kate Spade. With that spirit animal brand in mind, I created a blended logo mark for Coast Polish Co. The starfish symbol is evocative of relaxation on the coast. I searched for a serif font that would emulate the rhythm of waves. The typeface Ines won out over other serifs for its open apertures, its negative stress, and its tapered finals. The overall feeling is relaxed, rhythmic, and spacious. Ines' distinctive half-ball finals make it signature and memorable.

03 Color Palette

The feminine pink is balanced by the deep blue. The palette is rounded out by colors that take their cues from the sea and shoreline.

04 Typographic Palette

Ines was selected for its rhythmically rounded lowercase letters. Bergamo fills in for situations that require italics.

05 Pattern and Texture

Taking a cue from the starfish mark, the patterns and textures are organic, rhythmic, and beachy.

06 Packaging

The bottle, rectangular, provided a nice surface to spread a square logo across. The knocked out effect is a way to brand the bottle while still allowing the color of the polish to show through.

07 Advertisements

A holiday weekend sale necessitated an email blast. It was an introduction to the new brand and it needed to have the polish and structure of a high class retailer.
