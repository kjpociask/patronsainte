Brand and Business Cards

RokkinCat, a boutique development shop, transitioned from being savvy developers to trusted business consultants. They were in need of a brand that communicated that shift in offering.

They had a site designed, but it stalled before launch due to the effort required for the team to finish it. I felt it was key in their transformation. I selected and mocked up portfolio pieces, wrote copy, and resolved UX issues, working with the team until it was live.

After launch, we focused on business cards. Because the employees are selling their expertise, I felt that having a card that felt rare and established in the digital age would help them transition. Their 'Hand Coded Digital Products' tagline lent itself well to letterpress, hand printing. The cards have the gravitas needed to convey the level of care and attention they pay to their clients' business.

They debuted their new cards on Twitter with a photo, which had an engagement rate of 237% better than their average.
